Overview

ABB Ability™ solutions platform simplifies business operations in energy management in industrial applications, offering a range of digital tools for improved performance and growth enabling smarter decision making. The ABB Ability Marketplace™ provides access to cloud-based applications, reports, remote support services, and more, tailored to different industries.

As a product designer, I played a role in crafting the ABB Ability™ cloud base software solution for B2B marketplace, which aims to enhance supply chain collaboration, transparency, and efficiency within ABB's ecosystem. Alignment with stakeholders´ requirements and technical capabilities were crucial for ensuring the success of the project.
