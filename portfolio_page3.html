Overview

IFS is a leading ERP system that enables organizations to manage core business processes, including enterprise asset management, maintenance, and field service operations.

As a Product Designer in the Asset Management team at IFS, I worked closely with product owners, data scientists, and developers to explore how AI could automate data extraction from maintenance reports. Through this collaboration, we developed an AI-driven solution that enhances efficiency, improves accuracy, and streamlines maintenance workflows helping engineers make faster, data-driven decisions while keeping operations running smoothly.
