Overview

Worxmate is a comprehensive workplace management application designed to streamline collaboration, task management, and goal achievement within teams. Features such as setting OKR (Objectives and Key Results), task organisation, file management, and AI-powered assistance, Worxmate empowers users to enhance productivity, foster teamwork, and achieve their objectives effectively.

As product designers in this team, our aim is to leverage AI algorithms to offer intelligent insights and recommendations to users, ultimately enhancing their productivity and goal achievement. This case study showcases our journey in developing Worxmate, a collaborative workspace platform designed to streamline task management, facilitate effective communication, and drive productivity in the workplace.
